. Devices can be added to i-score, by clicking the "Add a device" button. This will let you choose the several options of your device, according to the communication protocol that it uses. . Devices will then appear in the explorer, and can then be explored hierarchically by browsing their nodes and subnodes, down to the addresses that hold actual parameters. . Selecting a node will select all descendants of this node. . Once a selection is done, clicking on "start" or "end" will take a snapshot of this selection, and assign it respectively to the start or end of the selected box.
